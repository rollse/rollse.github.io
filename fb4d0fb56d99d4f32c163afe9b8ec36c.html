How alcohol affects the sexual desire of men and women

Most people think of alcohol as an activator of sexual desire, but the truth is that it simply removes insecurities and social boundaries. Alcohol is mistaken for an aphrodisiac. in small amounts, it increases the desire for intimacy and sexual contact, helps to relax and feel comfortable. However, this effect is temporary, and in large quantities of alcohol has the opposite effect and reduces the ability to soberly assess the situation.

Influence of alcohol on men and their sexual desire.

Men's reactions to alcohol are individual, as it affects their bodies and minds in different ways. Alcohol suppresses the activity of the brain and nervous system and thus prevents the maintenance of a stable erection. This is mainly due to the fact that there are no chemical additives available substances needed to increase blood flow to the male genitalia. Drinking alcohol also reduces the production of male hormone in the male body. the male hormone is necessary for sexual arousal, and its reduced level affects the capacity of a man in bed. Thus, in General, men it is harder to achieve an erection and orgasm if they overdo it with alcohol. On the other hand, a light and cheerful intoxication increases a man's desire for sexual intimacy.

The influence of alcohol on women's sexual desire.

Alcohol has a similar effect on women, which leads to increased mutual satisfaction desires for intimacy. Women have a higher concentration of fat in their bodies than men, and as a result, alcohol begins to affect a woman faster, lifting her mood and liberating. On the other hand, alcohol prevents women from reaching orgasm.

Since alcohol distorts an adequate view of the situation, this can push a woman can do things that she usually never does. Thus, after consuming fun drinks, a woman can perform poorly predictable actions, such as having unprotected sex. Often cases of unwanted pregnancy are associated with alcohol consumption and idle behavior.
