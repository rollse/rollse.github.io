20 best dating tips

  1. 1.Date a few people at a time until you’re ready to become exclusive with one.
  2. 2.When words and action don’t align, pay more attention to his/her actions.
  3. 3.The three most important elements of a partner are kindness, consistency, and character.
  4. 4.Don’t make assumptions about your date- check out a person by getting curious.
  5. 5.Don’t bring your past relationships to your present relationships. Each person is a clean slate.
  6. 6.When a person first reveals him/herself to you, pay close attention. That’s when you learn most of what you need to know.
  7. 7.If a man is interested, he will pursue a woman. She doesn’t need to text the next day to remind him that they had a great time on the date.
  8. 8.During the courting phase, a woman’s job is to do nothing except say yes to a date, a first kiss, etc, etc, etc.
  9. 9.Let the man take charge if she wants a take charge man.
  10. 10.A woman doesn’t have to have a ‘perfect’ body and face to be attractive. An irresistible woman is fun, drama-free, intelligent, and sensual.
  11. 11.Don’t invest more into a man than he invests into you.
  12. 12.When you put someone on a pedestal, the only place he/she can look is down.
  13. 13.Enter a relationship with your eyes wide open. The character and values that you see are what you get.
  14. 14.Women need to stop hoping they can fix a man.
  15. 15.Don’t waste time in a relationship with someone who doesn’t value you.
  16. 16.Don’t plan the future before there’s a present.
  17. 17.Each date is an opportunity to learn something about yourself and about dating. Have fun and lighten up about dating.
  18. 18.As grandma said, every pot has a lid. Don’t give up hope.
  19. 19.Your self-esteem is equal to the quality of the person you attract.
  20. 20.Be the chooser. Don’t ever give up your power for a man or woman.
