25 tips on how to eat properly in your life

25 tips on how to eat properly in your life

If you are aiming for a healthy diet and proper nutrition, run through these 25 easy tips that you can use to improve your diet and dietary habits.

1. Replace fasting with food in moderation. You will be able to enjoy your favorite food and maintain your health, rather than risk it by using strict diets and missing important trace elements.

2. Instead of having a full meal, prepare something delicious. It will often be healthier and you will save a little moneys'.

3. Substitute meat for a vegetarian meal from time to time. This is good for the body.

4. Replace the orange juice with whole orange. When you drink juice, some of the fiber is lost, and therefore you lose the opportunity to take advantage of all the benefits of this fruit.

5. Replace shopping for fruits and vegetables in supermarkets with purchases at markets that are imported from dacha farms. The products of grandmothers on the market guarantee to a certain extent that they contain fewer chemicals than you can find in vegetables and fruits from stores.

6. Replace carbonated drinks with a glass of water.

7. Replace purchased seasonings and marinades for hand-prepared dishes. You can make a great sauce with a little lemon juice, pepper, and olive oil.

8. Instead of using the deep-frying method, take a non-stick frying pan and limit the fat. This way, the food will be just as delicious, but more healthy, and you will save money.

9. Replace the lollipops with frozen grapes. It's just as sweet and refreshing.

10. Replace regular tea with green. This will help clean the body and maintain a healthy weight.

11. Instead of overeating in a restaurant, take the uneaten food in a box home.

12. Eat more slowly to improve your digestive system.

13. Replace your morning fast food with healthy food. This is the most important meal of the day, so eat something healthy to set yourself up for a productive day.

14. Eat salad with spinach or Kale – they contain a lot of vitamins.

15. Use pasta made from unseeded flour instead of white flour to get more fiber.

16. Replace soda with tonic as a healthier alternative.

17. If you like pancakes and fritters, eat them with salsa instead of cheese. With salsa, they are just as delicious, but much healthier.

18. If you want less a bad snack while watching the movie, replace the chips with popcorn.

19. Replace beef with fish once a week. Fish is good for the heart, and you can also prepare very delicious dishes from it.

20. Instead of skipping meals and focusing on fast food, make sure you eat 3 meals a day per day without overeating.

21. Go to work with a meal plan instead of buying burgers - both cheaper and healthier.

22. Replace white rice with brown when cooking at home, it is healthier.

23. Replace mayonnaise with mustard if you want to preserve the flavor but reduce the number of calories.

24. Replace bacon for duck, if you like to eat meat, but salt less.

25. Use avocado instead of butter for the morning toaster for Breakfast.
